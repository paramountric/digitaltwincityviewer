This app shows how to signup and login into supabase using email and password.

When user come to the app, they should be presented with a login page where they can add their email and password.

If they don't have an account, they should be presented with a signup page where they can add their email and password, when clicking on the signup link.

When user clicks on the signup link, they should be presented with a signup page where they can add their email and password.

User will immediately get to the page behind the (protected) folder route. When signing up or logging in.

Use a dark theme with a feeling of digital twin cities, futuristic and a bit sci-fi.
